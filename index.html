Raisin Picker is a personal knowledge management tool built with Drupal (7.x).

A knowledge worker's tool kit

There is so much data, so much information to process. News, RSS feeds, search results, papers, books. Raisin Picker provides a tool box to manage what is relevant, to retrieve what is needed. It serves as an "outsourced braing", a memex.

Organization & Structure

Why not use Evernote, Google Docs or a simple text editor to do such thing? Well, you can. Raisin Picker focuses on structure and organize your information into nodes that are categorized by tags, connected with relations and rated by a number to indicate the relevance.

On the shoulders of giants

Most concepts applied are based on research and inspired by best practices of masters in their field. Beginning with Zettelkasten of Niklas Luhman, the concept of a memex, spated repetition.

Here is some more information about this project that is only revealed once clicked on.

Usability

Intuitive, responsive design using the Material Design Framework. Readable URLs. Create and edit content with a WYSIWYG editor (CKeditor). Colored icons representing rating and content types.

Dashboard

Configure your personal dashboard with the widgets you desire (e.g. recently created content, random content, charts, search box

Search

Full-text search. Filter by date, rating, tags, and type (faceted search). Using Apache Solr for indexing.

Reminders

For spaced reppetition, reminders are set automatically for you to review a piece of content after a month or a year. See also https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaced_repetition

Import & Export

Feed content by RSS feed. Mass import of existing data with Drupal tools. Export your data in CSV and JSON. Conduct bulk operations to edit/delete many content nodes at a time.

Statistics & Charts

Monitor created/updated content nodes. Count node views. Visualize in charts using Google Charts or Highcharts library.

Notifications

Connect to Pushover to get notifications on your device. Or simply get email notifications. Connect to IFTTT Maker to do even more based on the triggers you set.

History

Every edit and change is logged and can be undone. See your editing history. Count the number of views of a piece of content.

Relations

Connect nodes with each other. “Fieldable” relations.
